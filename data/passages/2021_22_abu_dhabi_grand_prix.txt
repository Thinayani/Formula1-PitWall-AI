Formula 1 — Abu Dhabi Grand Prix (2021)
Circuit: Yas Marina Circuit, UAE. Race date: 2021-12-12. Round 22 of the 2021 season.

Podium: P1 Max Verstappen (Red Bull), P2 Lewis Hamilton (Mercedes), P3 Carlos Sainz (Ferrari).
Max Verstappen driving for Red Bull won the race from grid position 1, completing 58 laps in 1:30:17.345.
Top 10 finishers: P1 Max Verstappen (Red Bull) — 26 pts; P2 Lewis Hamilton (Mercedes) — 18 pts; P3 Carlos Sainz (Ferrari) — 15 pts; P4 Yuki Tsunoda (AlphaTauri) — 12 pts; P5 Pierre Gasly (AlphaTauri) — 10 pts; P6 Valtteri Bottas (Mercedes) — 8 pts; P7 Lando Norris (McLaren) — 6 pts; P8 Fernando Alonso (Alpine F1 Team) — 4 pts; P9 Esteban Ocon (Alpine F1 Team) — 2 pts; P10 Charles Leclerc (Ferrari) — 1 pts.
Retirements/incidents: Sergio Pérez — Engine; Nicholas Latifi — Accident; Antonio Giovinazzi — Gearbox; George Russell — Gearbox; Kimi Räikkönen — Brakes; Nikita Mazepin — Illness.
